Better Directions



Welcome to Better Directions! We know your time is precious and being stuck in traffic is definitely not the best use of your time. Whether it’s running errands or your next vacation, let Better Directions help you by planning your next roundtrip.



Better Directions is a modern twist on the classic traveling salesman problem (where the salesman's objective is to find the shortest possible roundtrip). Better Directions takes live data from the Google Directions API to retrieve the best route and the app calculates the most time efficient way to visit each address.



Simply enter your address on the left sidebar and click “Calculate Route” and wait for the app to do its magic.



Note: Due to query limits of the Google Maps API, the requests are intentionally throttled to prevent errors. Requests of 6+ addresses may experience 1-2 minutes slowdown.
